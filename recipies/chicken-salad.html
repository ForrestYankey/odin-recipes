Chicken Salad

Discription

A simple chicken salad.

Ingredients

  • rotisserie chicken
  • mayonaise
  • green onions
  • parsley
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Remove chicken breasts from rotisserie chicken. Save remainder for another recipe.
  2. Pull or dice chicken then place in large bowl.
  3. Dice green onions and parsley and add to bowl. More or less depending on taste.
  4. Salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add just enough mayonaise to cover each piece of chicken.
  6. Mix well.
  7. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  8. Serve on bread or wrap and enjoy!
